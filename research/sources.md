# Research Project: Community-Led Growth for B2B SaaS

## Topic Selection

After reviewing the eight topic options, I chose **Community-Led Growth for B2B SaaS** for three reasons:

1. **It's the hardest moat to replicate in an AI-saturated market.** Cold outreach, SEO content, and LinkedIn posting are all increasingly automated and commoditized. Communities, built on trust, identity, and repeated human interaction, remain genuinely defensible.

2. **It matches the business reality of small B2B SaaS companies.** Companies at 100Hires' stage typically grow through relationships and word of mouth before they grow through paid acquisition. Community is the most honest reflection of that dynamic.

3. **The expert pool is less obvious.** Most applicants will likely default to LinkedIn or cold outreach because the loud voices there are easy to find. Community led growth practitioners tend to be operators and quieter writers, which forces more careful sourcing. That is exactly what this task is testing for.

## Approach

* Identify 10 practitioners who actually operate communities, not just write about the concept.
* Prioritize signal over volume: 10 high quality voices over 50 generic ones.
* Mix of formats: LinkedIn active operators, podcast hosts, long form writers, community platform builders.
* Collect their most recent and substantive content, not viral one liners.
